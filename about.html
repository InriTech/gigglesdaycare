Our Story

Nurturing Young Minds Since 20--

Founded in 20--, Giggles Daycare began with a simple mission: to provide a loving, faith-based environment where children could thrive in both English and Afrikaans. What started as a small home-based daycare has grown into a respected preschool serving families throughout Centurion.

Our name reflects the joyful atmosphere we cultivate every day. we've remained committed to our core Christian values while implementing the best practices in early childhood education.

Our Vision

To be Centurion's premier bilingual Christian preschool, recognized for excellence in early childhood development and for nurturing children who are confident, compassionate, and curious learners.

Our Mission

To provide a safe, loving environment where children develop spiritually, emotionally, socially and intellectually through play-based learning, Christian values, and bilingual immersion.
