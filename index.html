Who we are

About Giggles Daycare

Giggles Daycare is a bilingual Christian daycare located on Grace Ave opposite the primary school. We provide a safe, nurturing environment where children can learn and grow.

"Train up a child in the way he should go, and when he is old he will not depart from it." - Proverbs 22:6. This verse guides our approach to early childhood education.

Our Educational Approach

We follow the ELDAS curriculum which focuses on holistic development through play-based learning. Our bilingual program helps children develop language skills naturally through daily immersion.

Our Christian values are woven into daily activities through Bible stories, songs, and lessons about kindness, sharing, and love for one another.

Our Core Values

At Giggles Daycare, we believe in nurturing each child's spiritual, emotional, physical and intellectual growth. Our values include love, respect, integrity, and excellence in early childhood education.

We celebrate diversity and teach children to appreciate differences while finding common ground through shared values and experiences.

Our Facility

Our daycare features bright, spacious classrooms, a secure outdoor play area, and age-appropriate learning materials. Located conveniently opposite the primary school on Grace Ave, we offer easy drop-off and pick-up.

Safety is our top priority with secure access, first-aid trained staff, and strict hygiene protocols. Our kitchen provides nutritious meals prepared daily.
